Biography:
This is a very thorough biography on Bill Gates. Bill Gates is a super rich guy. He is the guy that made the company called Microsoft. Microsoft is a multibillion dollar company that made huge impacts on the world. This guy dropped out of Harvard to create this multibillion dollar company. He was studying computer science in college, but found that he could spend his free time more effectively by pursuing with his company rather than staying in school. He is no longer working for Microsoft. He is now a philanthropist.
